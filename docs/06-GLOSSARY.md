# Purpose

This glossary defines the official language used throughout the Health One project.


Its purpose is to ensure that product, engineering, AI, documentation, database, API, and UI all use the same terminology.


One concept should always have one official name.


---

# Language Policy

Official format:


> English Primary + Chinese Annotation
> +

Example:

> Service Record
> AI Summary AI
> Knowledge Entry

Future documents should follow this format whenever practical.

# Core Business Terms

## Customer

A person receiving health services.


---

## Health One Identity

The unique identity of a customer inside Health One.

Customer in Health One

---

## Health Profile

Structured long-term health information.


---

## Health Concern

The customer's current problem or concern.


---

## Health Goal

Expected long-term outcome.


---

## Service

A health management activity delivered by the store.


---

## Service Record

The factual record generated after each service.


---

## Feedback Record

Customer feedback after service.


---

## Follow-Up Task

Future action that should be completed.


---

## AI Summary AI

AI-generated structured summary.

AI

---

## Knowledge Entry

A reusable knowledge unit.


---

## Event

A meaningful project or customer activity.
