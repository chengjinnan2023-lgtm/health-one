# Health One

Health One is a long-term health management ecosystem project.

The Chinese concept name is:

> 健康元

Health One is not only a software product, mini-program, store SaaS, device booking system, CRM, AI chatbot, or marketplace.

Health One is intended to become:

> A continuously evolving personal health operating system and health ecosystem.

---

## 1. What Health One Is

Health One connects:

1. Customers.
2. Personal health identity.
3. Long-term health memory.
4. AI health companions.
5. Stores.
6. Health managers.
7. Services.
8. Devices.
9. Knowledge.
10. Ecosystem partners.

The core idea is that health should not be treated as a one-time service or transaction.

Health should become a continuous relationship supported by memory, trust, AI, real services, and long-term follow-up.

---

## 2. Core Concept: 健康元

健康元 is the customer-facing concept of Health One.

It represents a personal, continuously evolving health identity.

A customer's 健康元 may include:

1. Health goals.
2. Health memory.
3. Service history.
4. Follow-up records.
5. AI insights.
6. Store service context.
7. Long-term health growth trajectory.

The customer should be treated as the owner of their 健康元.

Stores may serve the customer with authorization.  
The platform may protect, organize, and analyze health information with permission.  
AI may assist based on authorized context.

## 4. Current MVP Direction

The current MVP should verify the shortest complete health value loop:

1. Customer creates or activates a Health One identity.
2. AI builds an initial health understanding.
3. AI provides personalized guidance.
4. Customer books or receives store service.
5. Store completes the service.
6. Service result is recorded.
7. AI follows up.
8. Health memory grows.
9. Customer returns.
10. The loop repeats.

The MVP is not the smallest number of functions.

The MVP is the shortest complete value loop.

---

## 5. Current MVP Focus

The early MVP should focus on:

1. 健康元.
2. AI health companion.
3. Store workbench.
4. Service or device connection.
5. Service record.
6. Follow-up loop.
7. Basic knowledge base.

The early MVP should not prioritize:

1. Full marketplace.
2. Complex franchise system.
3. Token mechanism.
4. Blockchain.
5. Full contribution monetization.
6. Full digital human system.
7. Overbuilt CRM.
8. Overbuilt ERP.
9. Premature large-scale automation.
