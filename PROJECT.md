## 3. Project Origin

Health One was born from a practical problem:

> Health services are often fragmented, short-term, product-driven, and difficult to sustain.

Many customers do not lack health information.  
They lack continuous health understanding, trusted guidance, and an executable service pathway.

Many stores do not lack products or services.  
They lack the ability to continuously understand customers, manage relationships, follow up outcomes, and improve operating capability.

Many platforms do not lack functions.  
They lack long-term memory, ecosystem coordination, and real service feedback.

Health One was created to address these structural problems.

## 6. Core Concept: 健康元

健康元 is the personal health identity of a customer inside the Health One ecosystem.

It represents:

1. The customer's health memory.
2. The customer's health goals.
3. The customer's health service history.
4. The customer's AI health companion context.
5. The customer's long-term health growth trajectory.

健康元 should grow over time.

Every service, follow-up, record, insight, and interaction may enrich the customer's 健康元.

## 11. Current Product Direction

The current product direction is to build the shortest complete health value loop.

The early loop is:

1. Customer creates or activates a Health One identity.
2. AI builds an initial health understanding.
3. AI provides personalized guidance.
4. Customer books or receives store service.
5. Store completes the service.
6. Service result is recorded.
7. AI follows up.
8. Health One memory grows.
9. Customer returns.
10. The loop repeats.

The MVP is not defined as the smallest number of functions.

The MVP is defined as the shortest complete value loop.
